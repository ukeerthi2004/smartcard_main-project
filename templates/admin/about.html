About SmartCart

A Premium & Powerful Smartcard E-Commerce Ecosystem

SmartCart is an advanced, secure, and modern web-based shopping and inventory management ecosystem. Powered by a robust Python-Flask backend and MySQL database, it bridges the gap between administrators and consumers, offering beautiful storefront interactions, secure multi-factor authentication, and streamlined management workflows.

Core Capabilities & Features

  • Secure Multi-Role Auth: Distinct admin and customer logins with robust password hashing and verification.
  • OTP Authentication: One-Time Password generation sent directly to email during sign-up for ironclad security.
  • Interactive Catalog: Beautiful customer storefront with full-search capability and dynamic category filters.
  • Real-time Shopping Cart: Seamless product additions, updates, and total price calculation.
  • Inventory Management: Complete administrative controls for products including creation, detail updates, and deletes.
  • User Profiles: Fully customizable user information profiles complete with custom image uploading.
